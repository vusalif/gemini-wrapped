Common Questions

Follow these quick steps to get your Gemini history:

  1. Go to Google Takeout.
  2. Click "Deselect all" to start fresh.
  3. Scroll down and find "My Activity", then check its box.
  4. Click the "All activity data included" button.
  5. Inside the list, click "Deselect all", then check only "Gemini".
  6. Confirm and create the export. Google will email you a .zip file when it's ready.

100%. Your data never leaves your device. All the processing, counting, and visualization happen locally in your browser. You can even disconnect your internet after the page loads and it will still work perfectly.
